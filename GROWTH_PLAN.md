# Brand BTS Affiliate Website Growth Plan

## Website Vision
Brand BTS will be a multi-category affiliate marketing website focused on helping users discover the best products, software, gadgets, fashion items, home essentials, and digital tools through detailed buying guides, comparisons, reviews, and curated recommendations.

The primary revenue sources will be:
* Amazon Associates
* Flipkart Affiliate
* EarnKaro
* VPN Affiliate Programs
* Hosting Affiliate Programs
* AI Tools Affiliate Programs
* Additional niche-specific affiliate networks

The website will prioritize SEO-driven content and long-tail commercial keywords that attract users with purchase intent.

## Content Types

### Commercial Content (70%)
*Examples:*
* Best Laptops Under ₹50,000
* Best Wireless Earbuds Under ₹2,000
* Best Women's Kurtis Under ₹999
* Best Air Fryers in India
* Best Hosting for Beginners
* Best VPN for Streaming

*Purpose:* Generate affiliate revenue.

### Comparison Content (15%)
*Examples:*
* Hostinger vs Bluehost
* NordVPN vs Surfshark
* iPhone vs Samsung
* JBL vs Boat Earbuds

*Purpose:* Capture high-buying-intent users.

### Informational Content (15%)
*Examples:*
* How to Choose a Laptop
* How VPN Works
* What to Look for in an Air Fryer
* How to Select the Right Office Wear

*Purpose:* Build topical authority and internal linking opportunities.

## Article Structure & SEO

### Standard Elements
1. SEO Title
2. Meta Description
3. Featured Image
4. Introduction
5. Product Comparison Table
6. Product Reviews (min. 1500 words, ideal 2500–4000)
7. Pros & Cons
8. Buying Guide
9. FAQ Section
10. Affiliate Disclosure
11. Conclusion
12. Internal Links

### SEO Checklist
* Primary & Secondary Keywords
* Keyword in URL, H1, first paragraph
* FAQ Schema & Product Schema
* Image Alt Tags
* Internal Links & External Authority Links
